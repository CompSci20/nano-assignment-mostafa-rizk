^X  : Exit nano
^W  : Search for a string or a regular expression
^K  : Cut the current line and store it in the cutbuffer
^A  : Go to beginning of current line
^E  : Go to end of current line
M-G : Go to line and column number (^_)
M-$ : Soft wrapping of overlong lines enable/disable
M-L : Hard wrapping of overlong lines enable/disable
